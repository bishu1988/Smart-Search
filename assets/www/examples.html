Simple Example

Suppose you wanted to find videos of goals scored by Eric Cantona, not involving Liverpool, between the years of 1993 and 1996, on Youtube. How would you google that?

With Smart Search it's simple, enter "Eric Cantona" in the "Main Search" field, type "goals" in the "Related Words" field, enter "www.youtube.com" in the "Website" box, make sure to exclude "Liverpool", and slide the "Year Ranges" to their appropriate positions.

Now simply click "Generate Query" and the result will look something like: "site:www.youtube.com ~goals -Liverpool 1993..1996 Cantona"

At this point unfortunately the user has to copy the resulting query into the Google search bar, using the copy and paste function on Android phones, due to Google's Terms and Conditions.
